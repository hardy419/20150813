Artist’s Statement

 

烏鴉雖然令人聯想到死亡，卻是一種成年後會回巢照顧父母的鳥，即中國人所稱的「孝鳥」。但在現今社會，有些人卻連烏鴉也不如。即使父母含辛茹苦把子女養育成人，得來的非但不是感恩卻是冷漠的對待。父母付出的一切，隨著時間的流逝而褪色，並被棄於腦海中的深處，被現在所遺忘……

Although crows may be associated with death, they are also known as “Birds of piety” in Chinese culture because they take care of their parents after grown up. However, nowadays, some people do not thank their parents for years of hard time raising them from birth, and also neglect their parents. Parents’ dedications faded gradually with time and were forgotten and abandoned…
