Artist’s Statement

 

現今有不少家長讓自己的孩子學習西洋樂器，他們漸漸融入西洋文化，而中國傳統音樂就不幸地被忽略了，甚至漸漸消失。我希望透過這幅水墨畫，喚醒大家對中國傳統文化的認知和重視，以免歷史悠久的中國文化從此消失。

Today many parents make their children learn western musical instruments. Western culture also gradually occupies their living. Chinese traditional music thus is being neglected that it may even disappear one day. I hope this Chinese painting can arouse people’s awareness and attention towards Chinese traditional culture in order to prevent the disappearance of the long history and valuable Chinese culture.
