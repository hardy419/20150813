Artist’s Statement

 

樹是作品中的主角，畫中情景是梨木樹邨中秋節的情況，居民一同拋螢光棒，樹上猶如掛滿點點星光，如此美景在其他地方不大可能看到。因此，我希望與更多人分享這一個美好的經歷。

The tree is the focus of the work. The scene took place during the Mid-autumn festival at Lei Muk Shi Estate. The residents there were throwing glowing sticks that look like twinkling stars hanging up in the tree. Such an incredible scene can hardly be found anywhere else. I therefore want to share this beautiful experience with more people.
