Artist’s Statement

 

到了2115年，香港這個城市還有人居住嗎？答案是沒有。這個地方的人全都到了地下生活去。因為地面已經沒有，甚至不可能再居住了。人們大多都認為地下居住會更優質，不會有天災影響，是一個安全的地方。但，你會願意選擇在這裡居住嗎？

By 2115, will there still be inhabitants in Hong Kong? No. All people here will live underground because the surface of the Earth will exist no more or become uninhabitable. Most people think that living underground offers a better livelihood which is free from natural disasters and is more safe. But, is that what you want?
