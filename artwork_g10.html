Artist’s Statement

 

這個作品的想法源自中國的詩詞──九言絕句。九字一行，四行為一首。而每句都有不同的對象︰第一句為母親，第二句為父親，第三句為朋友及第四句為情人。這四句所描述的內容在每個人的人生中都會出現，我只把木板塗上白色，帶出這些事情都是在無形的狀態下出現，以致很多人都忽略了。這亦是我選擇以雕刻形式做這件作品的原因。「我們對這些不留意，因為這些都是在無形中刻印在我們心中。」

The idea of my work comes from a style of Chinese poetry. Each sentence has its own target: The first sentence is about a mother, the second sentence is about a father, the third sentence is about a friend, and the fourth one is about a lover. We all experience what the four sentences describe. I white painted the wooden board (whole piece of work) expressing that all these matters happen shapelessly, thus being neglected. It is also why I used engraving to deliver the work. “We do not pay attention to these because they are invisibly engraved in our hearts.”
