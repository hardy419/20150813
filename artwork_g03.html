Artist’s Statement

 

我透過這套版畫表達對香港未來的期望，希望香港人能逃出各種問題。版畫展示香港人的過去、現在和未來。隨時間和環境的改變，他們對社會產生越來越多不愉快及不滿。我希望人們能爭取所求，能在未來有安穩快樂的日子。我用藍代表港英政府，紅代表中國政府，盼望2017年香港能在接受袓國國策與保留舊有核心價值中取得平衡。

I express my hopes to the future of Hong Kong that people will escape from different problems. This set of print shows the past, the present and the future of Hong Kong people. With the change of circumstances over time, they feel more unhappy and discontent. I hope people can fight for their needs and will have steady and happy life in the future. I use blue to represent British Hong Kong government and red to represent Chinese government. I hope in 2017 a balance will be struck between accepting the policies of our motherland and maintaining the core values of Hong Kong.
