Artist’s Statement

 

我採用齊白石展現個人意趣的風格，以意寫筆觸表現內心對大自然的真實情感；用渾樸的筆法及明快的色彩，展示純樸的童真。又參考黃永玉，拋卻傳統水墨的淡泊色調，改用鮮豔色彩，以俗寫雅，用入世方式寄寓出世思想。主題加入西方超現實的概念，畫中連一隻飛鳥也沒有，有如夢境般寧靜和奇異，以繪畫潛意識中的感受，畫出「中國的超現實主義」。我選定以四季來表達山水的變；選用卷畫，從左向右展開，代表四季的轉移。可是，以四季為題的水墨作品屢見不鮮，把四季畫於一畫中亦屬常有；故我只在一角畫上數抹粉紅，若隱若現，表示春季百花爭艷的日子已過去，只能懷念。佔了畫面一半以上的夏季，亦將至尾聲，從遠方可看見秋葉飄落，更可幻想冬日的冰雪。

I was inspired by Qi Baishi, using freehand style to express my feeling of the nature, as well as broad and crude brush strokes, and bright colours to show my innocence. Also referred to Huang Yongyu, I used vivid colours rather than the light tones adopted by traditional ink paintings to present the elegance of simple life. The idea of western Surrealism was also put into my painting. Without even a bird, the painting is as quiet and amazing as a dream, showing my inner feelings through the “Chinese Surrealist” painting. “Four seasons” is a common topic for paintings. Deliberately, only strokes of pink hinted the fading of spring. The large portion of summer was also approaching the end. While falling leaves could be seen in the far end, the white snow of winter should be on its way.
