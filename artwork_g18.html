Artist’s Statement

 

城市內，大家似乎每天過著正常的日子，對社會的安定與繁榮都很滿意，但其實人們都知道城市只有虛構的安定。面對這個虛幻的安定，我們卻視而不見，任由它——紅月亮——懸在城市上空，一直以來，沒有人感到詫異。

Everyone seems to live normally in the city. We are satisfied with the stable and prosperous society. However we all notice that there is an elephant in the room, allowing it, the red moon, to be hung in the sky without any surprise.
