Artist’s Statement

 

街道的霓虹燈熄了，世界漸變得冷清。 陪伴着我成長的舊區，也被社會的進步所取代。對比起舊時的夜夜笙歌，今天的街道，更顯冷清。儘管我盡力把它留在心中，但卻沒法挽回它獨有的味道——人情的味道。

Neolights on the streets are extinct. The world has gradually become cold. The old area where I was raised has been replaced by the advancement of the society. Comparing with singing and dancing in the past, the streets are so cold now. No matter how hard I try to memorise, I still cannot preserve the passion of the good old days.
