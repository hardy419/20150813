中學生視覺藝術創作展覽(2014/15)

由教育局藝術教育組舉辦的「中學生視覺藝術創作展覽(2014/15)」，已於2014年12月30日至2015年1月20日在教育局九龍塘教育服務中心舉行。

展覽目的是讓學生展示創意及視覺藝術能力，並促進學生、教師與公眾之間的交流。為鼓勵更多不同年齡的學生參展，藝術教育組把參展者分為兩個年齡組別，分別是「12歲至14歲組」及「15歲或以上組」。展覽設金獎、銀獎及嘉許狀，以嘉許學生出色的藝術表現。各獎項從每個年齡組別的「平面創作」、「立體創作」、「中國書畫」及「媒體藝術」中選出。

負責選出展品及得獎作品的評判有藝術界專業人士區潔愛博士（前香港教育學院文化與創意藝術學系副教授）、陳錦成先生（香港藝術發展局視覺藝術組主席）及鄧海超先生（前香港藝術館總館長)，以及藝術教育組代表。評選標準包括創意、美感、技巧與媒介運用，以及傳意能力。本年共有1,200件作品參展，當中約400件作品獲選展出，並且有20件作品獲金獎、20件獲銀獎及94件獲嘉許狀。所有得獎作品均在此網頁展示，歡迎公眾細心欣賞。

Exhibition of Secondary School Students’ Creative Visual Arts Work (2014/15)

The “Exhibition of Secondary School Students’ Creative Visual Arts Work (2014/15)” organised by the Arts Education Section, Education Bureau (EDB) was held from 30 December 2014 to 20 January 2015 at the EDB Kowloon Tong Education Services Centre.

The exhibition aimed to provide students with an opportunity to demonstrate their creativity and ability in visual arts, and promote communication among students, teachers and the public. In order to encourage more students of different ages to submit their entries, participants were categorised into two age groups - 12 to 14 years old, and 15 years old or above. In each age group, Gold Prizes, Silver Prizes and Certificate of Merits were awarded to artworks of four media, namely 2-Dimensional Work, 3-Dimensional Work, Chinese Painting and Calligraphy, and Media Art.

The adjudicators selecting the exhibits and awarded entries are art professionals, namely, Dr Eliza Au Kit-oi (former Associate Professor, Department of Cultural and Creative Arts, The Hong Kong Institute of Education), Mr Chan Kam-shing (Chairman, Visual Arts Group, The Hong Kong Arts Development Council) and Mr Tang Hoi-chiu (former Chief Curator, The Hong Kong Museum of Art), as well as representatives of the Arts Education Section. The selection criteria include creativity, aesthetics, manipulation of techniques and media, as well as communication. This year, around 400 pieces of artwork have been selected for exhibition from more than 1,200 entries. Among the exhibits, 20 pieces of artwork were awarded Gold Prize, 20 pieces awarded Silver Prize and 94 pieces awarded Certificate of Merit. The awarded artworks are displayed on this website.
