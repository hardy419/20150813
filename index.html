小學生視覺藝術創作展覽(2014/15)

由教育局藝術教育組舉辦的「小學生視覺藝術創作展 (2014/15)」，已於2015年6月26日至7月20日在教育局九龍塘教育服務中心舉行。

展覽目的是讓學生展示創意及視覺藝術能力，並促進學生、教師與公眾之間的交流。為鼓勵更多不同年齡的學生參展，藝術教育組把參展者分為兩個年齡組別，分別是「6歲至8歲組」及「9歲或以上組」。展覽設金獎、銀獎及嘉許狀，以嘉許學生出色的藝術表現。各獎項從每個年齡組別的「平面創作」、「立體創作」、「中國書畫」及「媒體藝術」中選出。

負責選出展品及得獎作品的評判有藝術界專業人士許明輝博士、楊懷俸先生、林曉東先生及陳志光先生，以及藝術教育組代表。評選標準包括創意、美感、技巧與媒介運用，以及傳意能力。今年共有1,193件作品參展，當中約500件作品獲選展出，並且有20件作品獲金獎、20件獲銀獎及118件獲嘉許狀。所有得獎作品均在此網頁展示，歡迎公眾細心欣賞。

Exhibition of Primary School Students’ Creative Visual Arts Work (2014/15)

The “Exhibition of Primary School Students’ Creative Visual Arts Work (2014/15)” organised by the Arts Education Section, Education Bureau was held from 26 June to 20 July 2015 at the EDB Kowloon Tong Education Services Centre.

The Exhibition aimed to provide students with an opportunity to demonstrate their creativity and ability in visual arts, and promote communication among students, teachers and the public. In order to encourage more students of different ages to submit their entries, participants were categorised into two age groups, “6 to 8 years old” and “9 years old or above”, for selection of awards. In each age group, Gold Prizes, Silver Prizes and Certificates of Merit were awarded to artworks of four media including “2-Dimensional Work”, “3-Dimensional Work”, “Chinese Paintings and Calligraphy” and “Media Art”.

The adjudicators selecting the exhibits and awarded entries are art professionals, namely, Dr Hui Ming-fai, Mr Yeung Wai-fung, Mr Lam Hiu-tung and Mr Chan Che-kwong, as well as representatives of the Arts Education Section. This year, about 500 pieces of exhibits were selected from 1,193 entries. Among the exhibits, 20 pieces of artwork were awarded Gold Prizes, 20 pieces awarded Silver Prizes and 118 pieces awarded Certificates of Merit. The awarded artworks are posted on this website, please enjoy viewing the works.
