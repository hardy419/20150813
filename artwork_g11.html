Artist’s Statement

 

作品展示香港存在的問題。畫面以香港區旗配上從我出生至現在所經歷過的事，以及從網上和報紙找到的熱門新聞，希望引起香港人的共鳴，同時表達我「生於斯，長於斯，死於斯」的意念。除此之外，我以紅色的文字襯托白色的區旗，帶出隱藏在香港的歷史。

This artwork shows the problems existed in Hong Kong. It consists of the Hong Kong flag and the text related to my experience from birth, and the hot issues found in the Internet and newspapers, hoping to arouse Hong Kong people’s echo and to express my feeling of “where I was born, where I grow and where I die”. In addition, I put the words in red on the Hong Kong flag in white to represent the hidden history in Hong Kong.
