Artist’s Statement

 

這件作品名為「Z世代」，現今不少孩子擁有平板電腦，使他們容易沉迷於電子產品，而舊日童年的玩意已經漸漸被遺忘。我希望藉作品令人們勾起小時候的回憶。

This work is entitled ‘Generation Z’. Nowadays many children have tablet computers, which easily let them indulge in playing with electronic products. However, games of my childhood have been gradually forgotten. I hope this work could help people recall their childhood memories.
