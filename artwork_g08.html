Artist’s Statement

 

我喜歡畫水彩畫, 馬蹄蘭也是我喜愛的花之一。它高佻、 堅強、挺拔。我剛學習繪畫裝飾畫, 便以此來表達「它」。

I like water painting. Calla lily is one of my favourite flowers. It is tall, strong and upright. I have just begun to learn decorative painting, and tried to express "it" in this style.
